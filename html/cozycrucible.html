What is Cozy Crucible?

Cozy Crucible is a safe jam-space in the Bel-Red district hosted by Wizard of Arc

Monday evenings from 6pm to 8pm (Pacific Time).

Some key points:

  • Treat everyone with respect.
  • Treat everyone's instrument with respect.
  • Don't be afraid to try new things out.
  • Bring your own instrument (a drum kit will be there).
  • If everyone who is present consents, sessions will video recorded.

To participate contact Wizard of Arc
